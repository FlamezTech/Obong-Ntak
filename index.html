About Us -

Obong University

Obong University is a prestigious private institution located in the serene and beautiful countryside of Obong Ntak, Akwa Ibom State, Nigeria. Established with a vision to foster academic excellence, moral integrity, and community impact, Obong University is committed to empowering students to achieve their highest potential through world-class education.


Our institution is built on strong Christian values, providing an environment where intellectual growth is seamlessly blended with spiritual and ethical development. At Obong University, we are proud of our diverse academic programs designed to prepare students for the challenges of a dynamic global society.


With a dedicated team of experienced faculty, state-of-the-art facilities, and a vibrant student community, we strive to nurture leaders who will excel in their professions and contribute meaningfully to their communities. Obong University remains a beacon of hope and a center of knowledge, equipping students to lead, innovate, and make a positive impact in the world.


Join us at Obong University and be part of a legacy of excellence, faith, and service.
